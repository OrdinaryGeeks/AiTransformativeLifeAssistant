You should work out isolatable sections of your body, like arms, legs, and abs once a week.
You should work your compound sections of your body, like chest, and back, once a week.
You should do cardio for 30 minutes 3 times a week.
You should rest one day a week.
If you work out your arms do biceps curl for 3 sets of 10 reps and tricep extensions for 3 sets of 10 reps.
If you work out your chest do benchpress for 3 sets of 10 reps and dips for 3 sets of 10 reps.
If you work out your legs do squats for 4 sets of 10 reps, leg extensions for 3 sets of 10 reps, and hamstring curls for 3 sets of 10 reps.
If you work out you abs do situps for 4 sets of 25 reps.
If you work out your back do lat pull downs 3 sets of 10 reps and do barbell rows for 3 sets of 10 reps.
